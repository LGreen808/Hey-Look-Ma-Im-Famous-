    Here is a black and white picture of John Ford

    John Ford was born February 1st 1894 in Portland Maine. His real name was not John Ford it was, John Martin Feeney. John Ford was Irish American with both of his parents being Irish immigrants. In his family he had 10 siblings and was the youngest child. When John Ford was young he was very interested in movies, he went to The Nickel (a movie theater) in Portland and would watch movies there. His older brother Francis Ford (originally Francis Feeney) was actually in the motion picture business before John and was also very famous before John got into the business as well.
